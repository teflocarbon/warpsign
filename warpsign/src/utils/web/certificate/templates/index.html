WarpSign Certificate Upload

Use this interface to upload your Apple Developer certificates for signing iOS apps.

Development Certificate

Upload your development certificate (.p12 file) for signing development builds.

Drag and drop your development certificate here, or click to select file.

A password is required with your certificate

Distribution Certificate

Upload your distribution certificate (.p12 file) for signing release builds.

Drag and drop your distribution certificate here, or click to select file.
